About the Event

The Research Scholars’ Day of IIT Palakkad is the annual flagship event of the IIT Palakkad research community. It celebrates the research contributions of scholars across diverse disciplines. It serves as a platform for researchers to showcase their work, engage in meaningful discussions, and foster interdisciplinary collaborations. The event features poster presentations, oral talks, and keynote sessions by distinguished academicians, creating an inspiring environment for innovation and knowledge sharing.

The seventh edition of Research Scholars’ Day marks the first time research scholars, postgraduate students, faculty, and industrialists are invited to be a part of this transformative event. The aim is to enhance the exchange of knowledge among researchers.

Pravaha beautifully encapsulates this essence. The term means "flow" in Sanskrit, symbolizing the vital process of knowledge exchange, much like a river that begins as a trickle and gathers strength through tributaries, ultimately merging into a vast ocean. This metaphor reflects how diverse research ideas converge, enriching the academic landscape and fostering innovation. Just as rivers nourish the land and sustain life, the flow of ideas nurtures intellectual growth and collaboration among scholars.
